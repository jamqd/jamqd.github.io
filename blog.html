Transitions

Death and consequences

It's not often that a single even will define the next few years of your life, but in many ways it is the experience myself and my fellow seniors will face in the not-too-distant future.

For many of us this means attending a university. While it's common for us to hear from adults that where you go will not define you and it's true, it is naive to believe that this experience will have no impact at all on our growth as people.

The people we meet, the classes we take, and the experiences we have will changes us. After all, why would anyone pay to go to school if there's nothing to gain, nothing that will make us better people.
